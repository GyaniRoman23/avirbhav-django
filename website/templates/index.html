AVIRBHAV

FIESTA FOR

RENAAISSANCE

The annual cultural and technical fest of the department, is welcomed by each and every student with immense eagerness. It is a carnival for the incredible talent dwelling in the college. The 20 day fest is inaugurated with a cause-oriented marathon followed by various sports, academic, formal and informal events that finally end in a spectacular cultural eve. The fest is autonomously organized by the students bringing in a great deal of sponsorship from several organizations. It lasts for some days and includes technology, programming, debate, dance, music, sports and other activities.
